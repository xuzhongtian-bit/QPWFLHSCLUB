description：

  • The Economics Club is a vibrant academic society dedicated to exploring the wonders of economics through engaging and entertaining approaches. We have designed a series of creative activities: the "Terminology Challenge" game makes specialized concepts like supply-demand relationships and marginal effects accessible to all; simulated investment competitions allow members to experience real trading in virtual stock markets; regular business case study sessions analyze economic phenomena through real-world examples. The club also features special events like "Economics Board Game Day" and "Entrepreneurship Sandbox" to make learning fun and interactive. Here, you'll not only systematically master fundamental economic knowledge, but also develop sharp market insight and rational investment thinking - laying a solid foundation for future personal finance and career development.
