club description：

  • "Welcome to join Yuzu Anime! (＾ω＾) Our club is dedicated to promoting anime culture and enhancing its recognition and acceptance within society. We aim to ignite members’ passion for anime, cultivate their interests and hobbies, and thereby improve their artistic appreciation and creativity. The club provides a platform for anime enthusiasts to communicate and share, facilitating the exchange of information and resources so that everyone can grow together through interaction.

    Our club activities are diverse and vibrant. We organize visits to various comic conventions for members to experience the latest trends and unique charm of anime; hold painting skill-sharing sessions where we discuss and learn anime-related drawing techniques together to improve members’ artistic skills; and regularly host anime viewing events, using the appreciation of classic works to help members better understand the meaning and artistic value of anime. In addition, we introduce anime-related knowledge and skills systematically through watching professional videos, allowing members to learn and grow in a relaxed and enjoyable environment.

    The club also actively participates in various school-organized activities, using anime culture as a unique vehicle to bring positive influence, enrich campus cultural life, and showcase the distinctive charm and value of anime culture."
