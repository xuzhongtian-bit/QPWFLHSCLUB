The Orcas description

proprieter：Peter

deputy director：Simba

adviser：Eric

description：

  • The Orcas, the band club of Qingpu World Foreign Language School, is a band that is not confined to a single style and dares to explore diverse musical expressions. We have tried many different music styles such as pop, rock, jazz, funk, punk, blues, classical, improvisation, and fusion, pursuing innovation and breakthroughs. In the process of music creation and performance, we emphasize teamwork, support each other, and make progress together. Whether it is technical exchanges or stage chemistry, we treat every rehearsal and performance with a professional attitude, striving to present the most sincere musical expression. This is a club for music lovers, where everyone can sincerely exchange music and experience the beauty and joy it brings. We believe that music is not only the display of skills, but also the transmission of emotions and thoughts. Through continuous adaptation and challenges, we hope to keep growing on the path of music and create our own music in the future, making The Orcas a well-known name.
