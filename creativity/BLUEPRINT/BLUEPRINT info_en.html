Blueprint description

proprieter：Rebecca

club description：

  • The BLUEPRINT Dance Club aims to cultivate artistic talents who love themselves, dance, and life. Its goal is to enrich individuals, the campus, and life. During their time in the club, members can inherit and promote various dance cultures; learn dance skills, improve physical fitness and artistic accomplishment. The BLUEPRINT Dance Club encourages members to express themselves bravely, showcase their charm, and emphasizes individuality. At the same time, it brings together members with unique personalities through dance, and on the basis of individuality, promotes teamwork to create outstanding dance works. The establishment of this club is of great significance for creating a good artistic atmosphere on campus, enhancing students' artistic accomplishment, and strengthening teamwork skills. It not only provides a platform for showcasing talents and exchanging learning experiences, but also stimulates students' creativity and expressiveness, boosts confidence, and promotes the healthy development of both body and mind.
