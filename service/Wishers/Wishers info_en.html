description：

  • "Wishers Community Service Society is a service-oriented organization. In today's society, problems big and small in the community are worth considering and solving, and the establishment of our society is also based on this. With the intensification of social aging, some elderly people will be sent to community nursing homes by their children. According to relevant research and some news reports, elderly people may lack companionship and entertainment projects, as well as professional medical assessments in terms of cognition. In response to this, our company visits nursing homes to bring the vitality of the younger generation to the elderly and help establish intergenerational communication; At the same time, provide cognitive assessment projects that are missing in some nursing homes to diagnose and intervene in a timely manner for the elderly. Our company also engages in public welfare activities related to rescuing stray cats, aiming to cultivate members' interest in public welfare, as well as their empathy and love for things."
