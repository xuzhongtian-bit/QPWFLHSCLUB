QPWFL Golf club description

proprieter：Tiger

deputy director：Angela, Edison

adviser：Bella

description：

  • "The Golf Club is a community that combines tranquility, strategy, and strength. We are committed to allowing this elegant sport to take root and flourish on campus, helping members improve themselves through focus and unleash their potential in nature. Golf is a sport that combines strategy, mentality, skills, and etiquette, and is known as the 'game on the green field'. It is not only a physical exercise, but also a tempering of the mind, cultivating calm and composed determination and the wisdom to look at the whole picture. Our daily activities include: Weekly routine training: Starting from scratch, systematically learn grip, standing posture, putting, cutting, and full swing. Simulated practical experience: Organize fun challenges using campus facilities to experience the combination of strategy and technology. Club cultural exchange: classic event viewing, equipment knowledge sharing, outdoor exchange activities. Here, you will reap: 1: An elegant skill that benefits for a lifetime! 2: Amazing focus and calm mindset beyond impatience! 3: A circle of close friends who love life and make progress together! 4: The great sense of achievement gained in every perfect swing! No experience required, no need to bring your own equipment, just bring your passion and patience! Whether you are pursuing a combination of movement and stillness, or eager to challenge yourself, the golf club welcomes you to join!"
