  • Volleyball Club is a student organization integrating competitive training, physical training and teamwork, and is open to all students who are interested in volleyball. The club is committed to building a proactive, enterprising volleyball training platform that is both technical and tactical, helping members improve themselves and gain friendship in sports. The club organizes training activities regularly every week, covering basic technical exercises such as passing, serving, spiking, and blocking. In a relaxed and pleasant atmosphere, everyone can master skills, but also enhance team awareness and reaction ability.
